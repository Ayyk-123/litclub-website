CODEX INSPICIO

Codex Inspicio is a book review competition on the book Farhenheit 451 by Ray Bradury. There is no particular constraint on the pattern of writing the review except that the word limit should be 800. You can write up your views about the theme of the book, or about the characters in the book and their traits. The review is then judged on the basis in which the review is written and upon your understanding of the story. There are also prices for this event worth Rs.1500 for the winners.

COOK A STORY

In this event, you are provided with a set of three visuals from which you need to cook a short story up. But there are rules and limitations! Firstly, the story must be an original composition. Ahough it can belong to any genre but there must be a clear connection between the story and all the three visuals provided and the story must have a title. There is no word limit. There are also prizes for this event worth Rs.1500 for the winners.

CACHE OF LEXIS

A one-of-a-kind event which requires you to wipe the cobwebs of your brain and exercise your mental faculties like you haven't done since your JEE! This event has a large participation every year. The objective is to crack codes, decode puzzles and connect all the dots, to reach the ultimate answer. If you are someone who loves solving problems, have keen observation skills and who can deduce answers to the most misleading problems; this is the chance for you to showcase your abilities. Of course there are hints everywhere around, it's just how close you look.
×

WORD WARS

Word Wars is the debating competition of IIT Indore. This event is a great opportunity for anyone who wants to improve his/her debating skills and enhance his/her performance in public speaking. But hold on because there are certain rules to follow. For each debate, there shall be 2 teams of 3 speakers each and the topic of the debate is given on the spot. The team in support of the topic is called the 'Government' and that opposes the topic is called the 'Opposition'. Each speaker is given 4 mins to speak in the alternaing order. First speaker is from the 'Government', then from the 'Opposition' and so on. Short questions and interjections are allowed. At the end, the winner team is given a prize money worth Rs.1500.

RANSENSE

Ransense is a creative writing competition of IIT Indore. So drill into the creative side of your mind and pick up your quills to carve a brand new piece of writing from scratch. A beginning paragraph is given to you and you will have to finish the story or you can even write a poem.
